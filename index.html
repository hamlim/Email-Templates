About Email Templates

Email Templates is a simple directory of both plain email templates and designer templates. This is a resource for anyone who wants quick and pre-made email templates to customize for individual use cases.

About Simple Email Templates:

Our offering of Simple Email Templates expands across several different use cases

Use Cases:

In many cases individuals or companies might need to send out large amounts of emails to many subscribers. Developing these email templates by hand for most individuals can turn out to be a pain. By using the simple or designer templates found here you or your company can quickly add your own content to the email and then send it off!

Email Templates are great for:

  • Crafting custom cold email's for job seakers, marketing departments, and sales individuals
  • Crafting weekly newsletters with updates about your product, service, or life
  • Sending custom beautiful emails to friends and family for special occasions
  • And more!
